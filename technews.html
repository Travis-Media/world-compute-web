NVIDIA Unveils RTX 50-Series GPUs

At the recent CES 2025, NVIDIA announced its new GeForce RTX 50 Series, powered by the Blackwell architecture. The lineup includes the flagship RTX 5090, RTX 5080, RTX 5070 Ti, and RTX 5070, all featuring DLSS 4 and fifth-generation Tensor cores. The RTX 5090, priced at $1,999, offers nearly double the performance of the RTX 5080, which is priced at $999. These GPUs are designed to leverage AI for enhanced graphics and performance, making them ideal for both gaming and 3D content creation. The RTX 5090 and RTX 5080 will be available starting January 30, 2025, with the RTX 5070 Ti and RTX 5070 following in February.
